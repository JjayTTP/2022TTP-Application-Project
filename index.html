Capstone Project: Implement a Technical Solution for a Small Business

August 11, 2021

This is the most recent project I've worked on. Check it out (and the other things I've done) in the "My Stuff" tab.

Representing what was learned in the Cisco Networking Academy - Networking Essentials course, this project simulates an IT company having obtained a contract position at a small advertising company called AnyCompany Corporation. Our contract responsibilities are to help them update their IT resources. For the project itself, we are to review their existing IT resources and produce a proposal that allows the company to take advantage of this new market.
