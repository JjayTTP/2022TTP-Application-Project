About Me

Welcome to my Nest

Though I was born in Brooklyn, New York, I embraced my passion for all things Creative when I moved to queens at the age of 7. Beginning with sketch art, I often sat and made my own versions of the cartoon characters I'd watched on television. That talent has never left me. From there I shifted my curiousity to the musical instruments that were played in my home. Now with my sights set on Software Engineering, I am constantly searching for ways to create the things that live within my imagination ...
